About DTDC

We are one of India’s leading integrated express logistics providers, operating the largest physical network of customer access points across the country.

Since our inception in 1990, we have evolved into a trusted courier and logistics partner, providing comprehensive, technology-enabled logistics solutions. Our Channel Partners play a vital role in generating business, building customer relationships, and ensuring seamless service delivery.

With over 14,000 access points servicing 96% of India’s population and international shipping to 220 destinations worldwide, we offer a range of solutions from traditional delivery to integrated warehousing and order fulfilment.
